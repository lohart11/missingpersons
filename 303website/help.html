The Fight How to help the fight

Learning how to recognize indicators of potential human trafficking can help save someone's life.


Some potential indicators include:
  • Visible injuries or illnesses
  • No legal documents
  • Restricted communication
  • Older partner
  • Unable to speak when alone
  • Owes money to an employer
  • Branding, such as a tattoo, on the neck or lower back

Available Hotlines Utah (801) 200-3443
National (888) 373-7888

The Hotline is equipped to handle calls from all regions of the United States from a wide range of callers including, but not limited to: potential trafficking victims, community members, law enforcement, medical professionals, legal professionals, service providers, researchers, students, and policymakers.

Questions To Ask
